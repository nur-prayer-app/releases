Daily Prayer Tracking

Prayer Times

Accurate calculation with live timeline, countdown, fasting tracker, and iqama offsets.

Fasting Tracker

Live Fajr-to-Maghrib countdown with progress bar. Auto-enables during Ramadan.

Qadaa Goals

Track missed prayers and fasting. Set goals, record progress, see completion estimates.

Extras

Log Duha, Shaf'a & Witr, Qiyam (with raka'at), and fasting — all tracked in stats.

Statistics

Islamic Reminders

Special days, Surat Al-Kahf on Fridays, and clickable hadith references with sources.
